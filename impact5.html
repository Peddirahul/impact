KERALA

Kerala, a state on the southwestern coast of India, is aptly nicknamed "God's Own Country." This tropical paradise boasts a captivating tapestry of landscapes, from serene backwaters fringed by palm trees to golden beaches lapped by the Arabian Sea, and verdant hill stations nestled in the Western Ghats. Kerala's tourism industry thrives on this incredible diversity, offering experiences that cater to every traveler's desire.Kerala is also known for its beautiful hill stations. Some popular hill stations to visit include Munnar, Wayanad, and Thekkady. Take a walk through the tea plantations, enjoy the cool mountain air, and take in the beautiful views of the surrounding landscape. Kerala is also known for its beautiful temples and palaces.
